Welcome to Plumtree Kennels

Established since 1990, Plumtree Boarding Kennels provides a complete service to ensure that your pet's needs are met in every way during their stay with us.

Going on holiday and need somewhere safe and comfortable to board your dog ? Want to ensure that they will receive individual care and attention?

Situated just outside Oswestry, in the heart of the countryside, our small, friendly, family-run business offers a peaceful setting for your pet's stay. Set in a quiet, secluded location, our comfortable and cosy boarding services are ideal for all breeds of dog, and will give you complete peace of mind that your pet is in safe hands.

Our experienced team are passionate about the loving care given to each and every pet - we ensure that every animal in our care receives individual attention and that they want for nothing whilst with us.

We aim to offer a flexible and affordable boarding service and are open 7 days a week for your convenience - we welcome visits and inspections and discussions about your pet's individual requirements.

A Happy and Relaxing Stay For Your Dog

Pets are part of the family, so they deserve the very best care and personal attention when they are away from home.

Being left in an unfamiliar environment and separated from owners can often be distressing and confusing for dogs, which is why we offer a first class kennel service to ensure that they feel safe, relaxed and looked after during their time with us.

No matter how many pets you have or how long you are away for, we have the necessary experience and facilities to ensure that they are completely safe, comfortable and cared for.

We fully encourage you to come and discuss your pet's requirements and look around our facilities, to optimise their comfort and your peace of mind. Where possible, we will always accommodate various diets, medication and living arrangement to make them as content as possible - you are also welcome to bring along their favourite items!

What you can expect from our facilities:

  • Fully heated accommodation
  • Immaculate condition
  • Covered outside runs
  • Comfortable, clean and cosy units
  • Regular exercise for your pet
  • We cater for various diets and medication
  • Regular attention and individual care
  • Quiet and relaxing environment
